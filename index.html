Soccerdb10 for-Activity

Human Activity Recognition

Soccerdb10 for-Activity is a multiview dataset for recognizing human activity in football videos. The proposed dataset consists of 32 high resolution videos containing 10 activity classes with 1629 videos (without augmentation) and 5500 with augmentation captured at 30fps and a resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels. The dataset augmented with several concerns like translation , rotation, flipping, cropping, zooming, changing brightness and more.The activity classes can be categorized as human-human interactions and human-object (Ball,Flag and card) interactions. The 10 activity are pass, dribble, head, tackle, shoot, punch, save, foul,plenty card and missconduct. This dataset will provide a baseline for recognizing human activity in football videos and will encourage the embedding researchers to progress the field.

Augmentation Tools

A Paid tool, Wondershare Filmora 12 is used to cut, crop and Augment the videos. Click on the link provided to know more about it.

The Process

All the foorball matches videos are dawnlod from the youtub,and use Wondershare Filmora 12 for cut, crop and augmentation (translation, flip, zoom and rotation)are provided for each videos . The augmentation are mapped back to the original videos for validating the dataset.

Representation

Augmentation are represented with the help of Translation and Rotate:

  1. Activity of players
  2. Activity class ID
  3. Translation and Rotate
